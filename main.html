Why I want to become a developer?

A software developer is a skilled programmer whose speciality lies in developing software for firms to accomplish a specific purpose. The created software can be a game, a database program, etc., and is meant to run on desktop or mobile. The software developer’s job is to write and run codes until the programs meet the client’s requirements. The position may be physically demanding, but the benefits of completing the tasks are well worth the effort. As a result, software development continues to be one of the highest-paying jobs.
